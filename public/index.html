EXPLORE EGYPT

While you’re in Cairo, take time to explore one of history’s greatest civilizations.

ANCIENT WONDER OF THE WORLD

Pyramids of Giza

Stand in awe before the last remaining wonder of the ancient world. The Great Pyramid of Khufu — 4,500 years old — rises from the desert just 30 minutes from the Four Seasons. And don't forget to ride a camel — it's a Cairo rite of passage.

EXPLORE →
THE WORLD'S LARGEST ARCHAEOLOGICAL MUSEUM

Grand Egyptian Museum

Opened in 2023, the GEM houses over 100,000 artifacts including the complete, unseen treasures of Tutankhamun. The dramatic glass-and-stone façade overlooks the Pyramids themselves — and at night, the experience is bathed in golden light.

EXPLORE →
BELLE ÉPOQUE, KHAN EL-KHALILI & RIVER VIEWS

Downtown Cairo & the Nile

Wander through Khedivial Cairo's grand Belle Époque boulevards, then lose yourself in the labyrinthine alleys of Khan el-Khalili bazaar. Sip mint tea at El-Fishawy Café — Cairo's oldest coffeehouse, open since 1797 — and end the day with sunset over the Nile.
