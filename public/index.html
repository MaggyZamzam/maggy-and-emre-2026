Explore Egypt

Discover the Magic

While you're in Cairo, take time to explore one of history's greatest civilizations. Here are our favourite places to visit.

Ancient Wonder of the World

Pyramids of Giza

Stand in awe before the last remaining wonder of the ancient world. The Great Pyramid of Khufu, 4,500 years old, rises from the desert just 30 minutes from the Four Seasons. And don't forget to ride a camel, it is a Cairo rite of passage.

✦ Arrive at dawn, the desert glows pink and the crowds are nowhere to be seen. Explore →

The World's Largest Archaeological Museum

Grand Egyptian Museum

Opened in 2023, the GEM houses over 100,000 artifacts including the complete treasures of Tutankhamun. The dramatic glass and stone façade overlooks the Pyramids themselves, and at night, the entire monument is bathed in golden light.

✦ Visit after dark, the illuminated façade is unforgettable. Explore →

Belle Époque, Khan el-Khalili & River Views

Downtown Cairo & the Nile

Wander through Khedivial Cairo's grand Belle Époque boulevards, then lose yourself in the labyrinthine alleys of Khan el-Khalili bazaar. Sip mint tea at El-Fishawy Café and end the day with sunset over the Nile.

✦ Visit Khan el-Khalili in the evening when the lanterns glow. Explore →

Green Suburbs & Open-Air Dining

Arkan Plaza, New Zayed

Arkan Plaza is the social heart of New Zayed, a beautifully landscaped open-air piazza of artisan boutiques, breezy outdoor cafés, and weekend farmers' markets. After sunset the trees and fountains light up and the whole plaza glows.

✦ Go in the evening when the plaza lights come on, it is at its most magical. Explore →
